The FIFA World Cup is the biggest international football tournament in the world. It is organized by the Fédération Internationale de Football Association, the international governing body of football.
The first FIFA World Cup was held in 1930 in Uruguay. Uruguay became the first champion by defeating Argentina in the final.
The FIFA World Cup is held once every four years, making it one of the most anticipated sporting events in the world.
National teams from around the world compete in qualification tournaments to earn a place in the World Cup finals. From 2026, the tournament features 48 teams instead of 32.
The tournament begins with a group stage, where teams are divided into groups and play round-robin matches. The top teams then advance to the knockout stage.
The knockout stage includes the Round of 32 (from 2026), Round of 16, quarterfinals, semifinals, and the final. A team must win each match to stay in the competition.
Different countries host the FIFA World Cup. The 2026 FIFA World Cup is jointly hosted by United States, Canada, and Mexico.
The winning team receives the famous FIFA World Cup Trophy, which is made of 18-carat gold and is one of the most prestigious trophies in sports.
Brazil is the most successful team in World Cup history, having won the championship five times.
Argentina won the 2022 FIFA World Cup by defeating France in an exciting final held in Qatar.
Many legendary football players have made history in the World Cup, including Pelé, Diego Maradona, Lionel Messi, and Cristiano Ronaldo.
Individual awards such as the Golden Ball, Golden Boot, Golden Glove, and Best Young Player Award are presented to outstanding performers during the tournament.
The FIFA World Cup attracts billions of viewers worldwide and is one of the most watched sporting events, bringing together fans from every continent.
The tournament promotes sportsmanship, teamwork, cultural exchange, and unity among nations while inspiring young athletes to pursue football.
The FIFA World Cup is considered the highest honor in international football because it showcases the world's best national teams and creates unforgettable moments that are remembered for generations.
